Why Heavens exists

To make the night sky feel connected: beautiful to look at, clear to learn from, and rich enough to keep exploring.

Heavens exists because astronomy becomes more meaningful when visual wonder, scientific explanation, and discoverable learning routes all reinforce each other. The goal is not just to show objects, but to help people understand why they matter and where to go next.
